Cloudstone SMP

Cloudstone SMP is a server where players can survive but if they die they lose a heart Lifesteal. Join us if you want a challenging experience with no hackers. Do you want a hacker free server? Join our staff team and help us prevent hackers.

Our goal is to make sure players enjoy a new experience with different & unique features. Join or Create your own team, build a team & battle other teams on the server. Become the richest & strongest team on the server.

Written & Published by ScriptureDevelops

Server Rules

  • Treat members with respect we do not tolerate bullying, harassment, or racism.
  • Any form of hacking/cheating is strictly prohibited and will result in a ban.
  • Do not spam, advertise, or use offensive language.
  • Keep gameplay fair no combat logging or heart glitches.
  • Follow staff instructions and maintain a positive environment.
  • Use common sense; if you think something isn’t allowed, don’t do it.
